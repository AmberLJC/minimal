About me

Greetings~~ My name is Amber(Jiachen) Liu. I love taming big data, building system and applying machine learning. I am almost always smiling. :)

I am senior undergraduate student at University of Michigan and Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am doing double major in Data Science and ECE respectively with Math minor. My research interest mainly falls on the database system and system for machine learning.

Right now, I am working on scheduling hyperparameter tuning system in Umich EECS advised by Prof. Mosharaf Chowdhury . Previously, I am also working on image retrieval in MIT CSAIL advisoed by Prof. Sam Madden. Also, I was working on share-ride route recommendation in Umich IOE advised by Prof. Siqian Shen. I really had a great time working in these groups, yay!
