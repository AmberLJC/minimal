About me

Greetings~~ My name is Amber (Jiachen) Liu, fourth year CS PhD advised by Prof. Mosharaf Chowdhury. My research focuses on resource management for machine learning applications = {AutoML, FL, LLMs}. I have maintain paper lists for Federated Learning (FL) systems and Large Language Models (LLMs) systems. I am open to discussing research ideas and seeking academic collaborations. Drop me email if interested!

Before, I had a great time working with Prof. Sam Madden and Prof. Siqian Shen, yay! I am almost always smiling. :)
